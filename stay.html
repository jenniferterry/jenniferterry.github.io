Where to stay

Three of the best places to stay in Paris on a budget

Even if you are on a tight budget, there are lots of accommodation options in Paris. You could check out AirBnB for homely stays in brilliant locations, consider a hostel stay if you are travelling alone, or even check out sofa surfing options if you are open to new experiences.

We have found three of our favourite accommodation options in Paris which we have loved when we have travelled on a budget, and here they are:

  • 1. St Christopher’s Inn, Paris Gare du Nord
      • With dorm beds from £50 per night and private rooms from £80, this is a great lodging option for guests wanting to be close to public transport and the city centre. What is more, the hotel boasts a bar, restaurant and complimentary breakfast.
    Image 1
  • 2. Hotel Ibis Budget Chatillon, Paris Ouest
      • With rooms for two people from £60 per night, including breakfast, this hotel is ideal for couples. Each room has a flat-screen TV, perfect for watching films and relaxing after a busy day in Paris. The hotel also boasts parking which is ideal if you are travelling with a car.
    Image 2
  • 3. B&B Hotel Paris, Porte des Lilas
      • A contemporary budget hotel that has streamlined rooms on offer from £37 per night. The hotel is close to public transport links, and is within 30 minutes of Paris’ best attractions. A great option for those wanting to explore the city but stay in a quiet location.
    Image 3
