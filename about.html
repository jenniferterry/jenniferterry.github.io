About

“Paris is always a good idea.” – Audrey Hepburn

It’s no secret that we love Paris. Not only does the city have loads to offer outside including the Eifel tower, and the Seine River, it also boasts one of the world’s most interesting museums, and incredible churches. So, no matter the weather or time of year, you are sure to love Paris.

We have visited more times than we can count, and feel that with each visit our love for the city grows and grows. We have developed this website to inspire you to visit our favour place. We have worked hard so that your trip to Paris is as easy as it can be. We have found great places to stay, the top 10 things to do, and have developed a list of phrases to help you get around.

“A walk about Paris will provide lessons in history, beauty, and in the point of Life.” – Thomas Jefferson
